Narrative
A breaking story surfaces. Before reading a word, Alex is evaluating the source. Format and origin determine whether they engage or scroll past.
Alex actively searches trusted outlets. Goal: triangulate. If two trusted sources agree on facts, the story is real. Any friction here terminates the visit.
Moves from verification to comprehension. Seeks synthesis and implications. When synthesis is found, engagement is high. When stuck in chronological updates, frustration spikes.
Understanding forms away from the screen — in conversations, commutes, ambient processing. The test happens in conversation: does Alex's synthesis hold up when challenged?
Alex decides: act, share, follow, or close. The follow/subscribe decision — at peak trust, at the end of a valued piece — is the highest-leverage loyalty moment. Most platforms miss it.
Reviewing whether the media diet served them this cycle. The source opened first in the next cycle is the source that consistently earned trust in this one.

Problems
  • Urgent framing activates distrust before content is read
  • Notifications optimised for clicks signal manipulation
  • Podcast too long for the alert moment
  • Trusted source hasn't published yet — stuck with less trusted version
  • Paywall before sourcing visible = trust block
  • Divergent framing across trusted sources increases anxiety
  • Breaking news format optimised for speed, not comprehension
  • Podcast lags behind the news cycle
  • Commentary that becomes advocacy breaks trust mid-read
  • Incomplete understanding means anxiety persists
  • Understanding that fails in conversation sends Alex back to research
  • Sharing from less-trusted source = reputational risk — suppresses sharing
  • Civic actions disconnected from the specific story
  • Closing CTAs ("donate to newsroom") feel tone-deaf
  • Follow/subscribe mechanism buried — loyalty moment missed
  • No way to gauge whether consumption produced understanding
  • Re-engagement notifications resist the break Alex needs
  • Most platforms make retrospective source comparison impossible
Opportunities
  • Calm, sourced headlines break through skepticism
  • Newsletter format signals editorial curation before opening
  • Show notes at notification time serve the breaking moment
  • First among trusted sources at verification = confirmation source positioning
  • Sourcing visible before paywall earns credibility before the ask
  • Explaining why framings differ earns analytical trust
  • What we know / don't / means — structure earns repeat visits
  • Same-day synthesis earns the depth moment and the memory of understanding
  • Evidence-forward commentary earns trust even when Alex disagrees
  • Anticipating counter-arguments makes Alex conversation-ready
  • Visible credibility signals make sharing a lower-risk act
  • Why-this-matters framing reduces ambient cognitive load
  • Local actionable civic step connected to the specific story
  • Closing state that gives permission to disengage builds long-term trust
  • Follow CTA at end of valued piece = highest-leverage loyalty capture
  • Comprehension-oriented product design is distinctive in market
  • Cycle-seam content builds relationship vs. manufacturing urgency
  • Visible reliability track record earns permanent rotation position
