## Section 2: Solution Hypothesis

The Contact Centre Transformation Program proposes three interlocked workstreams that together address the root causes of the current failure state — not the symptoms:

**Workstream 1 — Agent Empowerment:** Provide frontline staff with integrated tooling (single system view of the customer), expanded authority to resolve the most common issue types without escalation, and structured coaching. *This reduces AHT by eliminating system-switching time and reduces repeat contacts by improving resolution quality in the first call.*

**Workstream 2 — Process Redesign:** Conduct root-cause analysis of the top 5 repeat-contact drivers. Redesign the end-to-end process for each. Implement process changes with supporting knowledge base updates and agent training. *This directly targets the 34% repeat contact rate by fixing the processes that generate it.*

**Workstream 3 — Digital Deflection Pathway:** Build self-service capability for the contact types that are deflectable (billing queries, status checks, standard changes). This reduces total call volume for interactions that don't require human handling, reducing AHT and freeing agent capacity for complex interactions they can resolve well.

This program is preferred over alternatives because: (a) a technology-only solution (e.g., IVR upgrade) addresses containment but not resolution quality; (b) training alone without process or tooling change produces short-term improvement that decays; (c) the three workstreams are interdependent — empowering agents without fixing the processes they work within, or deflecting volume without improving resolution of what remains, produces sub-optimal results.

### Risk to Value

The value case is most sensitive to: (a) the AHT reduction assumption — if process issues outside the contact centre (e.g., upstream digital experience) are driving AHT, our interventions will have less impact than projected; (b) the agent attrition driver — if attrition is primarily compensation-driven rather than tools/experience-driven, the program will not move the needle on retention.
