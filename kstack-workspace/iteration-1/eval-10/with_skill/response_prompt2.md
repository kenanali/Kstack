### Why the system inventory matters here specifically

Insurance claims are a journey where **the backstage is the product**. Unlike a retail or digital experience, what the customer experiences is almost entirely determined by what's happening in systems and processes they can't see:

- Whether their claim is automatically triaged or sitting in a human queue
- Whether the assessor has access to their full policy history or is working from a partial record
- Whether the payment system is integrated with case resolution or requires a separate manual trigger
- Whether decline letters are generated from a template or personalised to the specific claim basis
