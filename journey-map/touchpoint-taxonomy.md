# Touchpoint Taxonomy

A reference vocabulary of customer touchpoints organized by channel type. Used by the `/journey-map` skill to ensure consistent, precise touchpoint naming.

---

## DIGITAL CHANNELS

**Website**
- Homepage / landing page
- Product or service information pages
- Pricing page
- FAQ / help centre content
- Account creation / registration flow
- Login page
- Online application or form
- Order confirmation page
- Account dashboard / overview
- Document download (statement, policy, report)

**Mobile App**
- App onboarding flow
- Home screen / dashboard
- Transaction or activity feed
- Feature-specific screens (payment, booking, search)
- Push notification (promotional, transactional, alert)
- In-app message or banner
- App error or empty state screen
- Settings and preferences

**Email**
- Welcome / onboarding email
- Transactional confirmation (order, booking, payment)
- Account alert or notification
- Billing or invoice email
- Marketing or promotional email
- Re-engagement email
- Feedback or survey request
- Service or policy update notification

**SMS / Messaging**
- Transactional SMS (confirmation, OTP, alert)
- Appointment reminder
- Promotional SMS
- WhatsApp or RCS messaging
- Chatbot conversation via messaging platform

**Live Chat / Chatbot**
- Website chatbot (automated)
- In-app chatbot
- Live agent chat (web)
- Live agent chat (app)
- AI assistant conversation

**Social Media**
- Organic brand post (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, X)
- Paid social advertising
- Direct message (DM) from customer to brand
- Brand comment reply
- Community or group page post
- Social proof / review shared via social

**Self-Service Portal**
- Online account management
- Claims or application portal
- Order tracking portal
- Complaint submission form
- Knowledge base / troubleshooting guide
- Video tutorial or how-to content

**Online Advertising**
- Search ad (Google, Bing)
- Display / banner ad
- Social media paid ad
- Retargeting ad
- Sponsored content

---

## HUMAN CHANNELS

**Contact Centre — Inbound**
- IVR (Interactive Voice Response) menu
- Queue / hold experience (including hold music, messages)
- Agent greeting and initial triage
- Agent conversation — information gathering
- Agent conversation — problem solving
- Agent conversation — outcome delivery
- Call wrap-up and confirmation
- Post-call survey

**Contact Centre — Outbound**
- Proactive outbound call (service, retention, collections, sales)
- Automated outbound call / robocall
- Callback (scheduled or immediate)

**In-Person — Branch / Store / Office**
- Physical environment on arrival (signage, queuing, ambience)
- Greeter or receptionist interaction
- Waiting experience
- Staff consultation or service delivery
- Transaction completion
- Farewell interaction
- Physical promotional materials (brochures, displays)

**Field Service / Home Visit**
- Appointment confirmation and pre-visit communication
- Technician or agent arrival
- Service delivery in customer's environment
- Explanation and handover
- Post-visit follow-up

**Relationship Manager / Account Manager**
- Proactive check-in call
- Review meeting (in person or video)
- Response to query or request
- Advice or recommendation conversation
- Contract renewal discussion

**Event / Exhibition / Roadshow**
- Brand stand or exhibit
- Presentation or demonstration
- Staff interaction at event
- Follow-up after event

---

## PHYSICAL / ENVIRONMENTAL CHANNELS

**Physical Environment**
- Store / branch exterior and entrance
- Interior layout and wayfinding
- Ambience (lighting, sound, cleanliness)
- Queue management (physical)
- Signage and information displays
- Point of sale / checkout area

**Physical Materials**
- Product packaging
- Unboxing experience
- User manual or quick start guide
- Welcome pack (physical)
- Printed statement, invoice, or letter
- Contract or agreement document (physical)
- Renewal or cancellation letter

**The Product / Device Itself**
- First use experience
- Ongoing interaction with product
- Product failure or breakdown
- Product end-of-life or disposal

---

## AUTOMATED / SYSTEM CHANNELS

**Billing & Payments**
- Invoice / bill (digital or physical)
- Payment confirmation
- Failed payment notification
- Direct debit confirmation
- Refund notification

**IVR & Automated Voice**
- IVR menu navigation
- Automated appointment reminder call
- Fraud or security alert call
- Automated status update call

**System Notifications**
- Account activity alert
- Threshold / limit alert
- Policy or product change notification
- Security notification (login, password change)
- Maintenance or outage notification

**Loyalty & Rewards**
- Points / rewards balance notification
- Tier change communication
- Offer or reward notification
- Expiry warning

---

## THIRD-PARTY CHANNELS

**Review Platforms**
- Google Reviews
- Trustpilot
- App Store (Apple / Google Play) ratings and reviews
- Industry-specific review sites
- Social proof widgets on website

**Comparison & Aggregator Sites**
- Price comparison sites (e.g., Compare the Market, Finder)
- Industry aggregators (e.g., Trivago, Booking.com)
- Broker or advisor recommendation

**Word of Mouth**
- Personal recommendation from friend, family, colleague
- Online community recommendation (Reddit, Facebook group, forum)
- Influencer or media mention

**Partner / Reseller Touchpoints**
- Partner sales conversation
- Partner onboarding experience
- Partner-delivered service interaction
- Co-branded materials

**Media & PR**
- News article or broadcast coverage
- Analyst or industry report mention
- Corporate announcement (press release, investor communication)
